About Rong Lungma

A tranquil valley is far from city and villages...
Dotted with few homes...
In the valley there is a small stream gurgling with fresh glacial water...
Around it lands are cultivated...
Surrounded by mountains having varied hues and textures...
The locals call it “Rong Lungma” (the faraway valley...)
The houses are ancient, built with stone...
Each being a witness to generations...
It is the valley of silence and peace...
It is the valley of flaming mountains...
It is the valley which I call my home...

The valley resides in the Trans Himalayan mountains, in the highland of Ladakh in Northern India

About Me

I am Norphal, a native to Ladakh, a dreamer, a doer, an avid learner, a nature lover. I am about to embark on a journey of self-discovery and self-education by re-cultivating the valley and turning it into an example of sustainable living. And I would love you to join me for part of the journey.
